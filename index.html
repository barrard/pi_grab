James Charisma is an award-winning writer and editor from Honolulu, covering stories in the fields of arts, entertainment, and pop culture. I have degrees in Psychology, Criminology, and Criminal Justice from the University of Hawaii at Manoa and Chaminade University, specializing in forensic and psychopathology, behavioral analysis, abnormal personality profiling, and conducting crime scene investigations.

His interests and passions include the opera, travel, playing with kittens, and eating cheeseburgers.
